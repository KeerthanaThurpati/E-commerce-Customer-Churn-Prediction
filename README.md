# 🛒 E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an e-commerce customer dataset to understand customer behavior, engagement patterns, purchasing trends, and churn factors.

The goal is to extract **meaningful insights** that can help businesses improve customer retention, increase sales, and enhance user experience.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains customer-related features such as:

- Demographics: Age, Gender, Country, City  
- Engagement: Login_Frequency, Session_Duration_Avg, Pages_Per_Session  
- Purchase Behavior: Total_Purchases, Average_Order_Value, Wishlist_Items  
- Customer Actions: Cart_Abandonment_Rate, Returns_Rate  
- Financial Metrics: Credit_Balance, Lifetime_Value  
- Marketing Metrics: Email_Open_Rate, Discount_Usage_Rate  
- Support Metrics: Customer_Service_Calls  
- Other: Churned, Signup_Quarter  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand **customer engagement patterns**
- Identify **factors affecting purchases**
- Detect **churn behavior and high-risk customers**
- Analyze **conversion gaps**
- Provide **business-driven insights**

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Univariate Analysis
- Distribution of Age, Login Frequency, Purchases
- Country and Gender distribution

### 🔹 Bivariate Analysis
- Login Frequency vs Total Purchases  
- Age vs Membership Years  
- Wishlist Items vs Purchases  
- Country vs Purchases  

### 🔹 Multivariate Analysis
- Engagement vs Purchases vs Country  
- Customer Service Calls vs Cart Abandonment vs Churn  
- Pages per Session vs Session Duration vs Purchases  
- Reviews & Social Engagement vs Lifetime Value  

---

## 🔥 Key Insights

- Customer **engagement strongly influences purchasing behavior**
- **Login frequency** has a positive impact on total purchases
- High **cart abandonment and service calls** lead to churn
- **Wishlist activity does not always convert** into purchases
- Customer behavior is **consistent across countries**
- Demographic features (Age, Gender) have **minimal impact**
- **High engagement + low purchases** indicates conversion issues
